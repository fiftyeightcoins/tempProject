Themes in Indian History - Part I (NCERT)

Class 12 | Ancient Indian History

Book Overview

This book explores the ancient period of Indian history, focusing on the early societies, kingdoms, empires, and the cultural development of the Indian subcontinent.

Themes / Chapters Covered

  • Chapter 1: Bricks, Beads and Bones – The Harappan Civilization
  • Chapter 2: Kings, Farmers and Towns – Early States and Economies (c. 600 BCE - 600 CE)
  • Chapter 3: Kinship, Caste and Class – Early Societies
  • Chapter 4: Thinkers, Beliefs and Buildings – Cultural Developments

Purpose of the Book

The book is designed to help students understand the complexity of early Indian history through archaeological and textual sources, and explore how historians reconstruct the past.
